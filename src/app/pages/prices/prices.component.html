Policies

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 48+ hours before check-in: full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours: 50% of the booking fee. No-shows: no refund.

Vaccination Requirements

All dogs must be current on: Rabies, DHPP (Distemper, Hepatitis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza), and Bordetella. We require proof of vaccinations before the first stay.

Late Pick-Up

Pick-up after 12:00 PM on the scheduled end date incurs a half-day charge ($35). Pick-up after 6:00 PM incurs a full additional night charge.

Capacity

We limit capacity to ensure quality care. Maximum 5 dogs per service type at any time. Advanced booking is recommended, especially for holidays.

Health & Safety

Dogs must be free of contagious conditions. We reserve the right to decline or discontinue care if a dog poses a risk to staff or other guests.
